* * * ASSIST OFFICER PROCEDURE * * *

  • IMMEDIATELY - re-transmit the request on the zone, region AND Emergency talkgroups in the following manner:

    ALL UNITS - OFFICER NEEDS ASSISTANCE - [location] & [nature]

  • Dispatch FIVE (5) additional units to the event
  • Copy the event via CAD to all surrounding self-dispatched agencies
  • If additional requests for assistance are received for the same event, dispatch TEN (10) additional units to the event for each additional request.
    • Ensure that ALL surrounding self dispatched agencies are notified of the additional requests.
