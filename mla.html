TRAUMA WITH INJURY

  • If unconscious, go to AIRWAY CONTROL (Trauma).
  • Have the patient lie down and keep calm.
  • Do not remove or touch an impaled object.
  • Do not touch weapons or disturb the scene.
  • If bleeding, use clean cloth and apply pressure directly over it.
  • If the cloth becomes soaked, do not remove it add another cloth to what is already there.
  • Tell the patient not to change clothing, bathe, or shower.
  • Keep the patient warm.
  • Do not disturb anything.
  • Gather the patient's medications, if any.
