ALL CALLERS INTERROGATION
  1. Where is the location of the emergency? (Address or location)
  2. What is the problem?
  3. What is your name?
  4. Is the patient conscious? (Able to talk, awake)
    • YES: Determine the Chief Complaint, sex and age and turn to appropriate card.
    • NO: Continue.
  5. Is the patient breathing NORMALLY?
    • YES: Go to Unconscious / Syncope card.
    • Unsure: Describe the patient's breathing or put the phone by the patient so I can hear it. (If Agonal breaths are heard, code as MCA, go to step 6)
    • NOT BREATHING: Code as MCA, go to step 6.
  6. Help has been dispatched, we are going to do CPR
              Go to the appropriate CPR Card
