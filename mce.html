CHEST PAINS

  • If patient is not breathing, determine appropriate age group and go to CPR.
    [ ADULT CPR --- CHILD CPR --- INFANT CPR ]
    1. ave the patient sit or lie down, whichever is more comfortable.
      (If Patient is pregnant, DO NOT administer aspirin or nitroglycerin - go to step 4.)
    2. Aspirin Administration:
      • Has the patient vomited blood (coffee ground-like material) or passed any dark/tarry stools?
        • If YES: Do not give aspirin.
        • If NO:Is the patient able to take aspirin?
          • If YES: Have the patient chew four baby aspirin (81 mg)
            or one adult aspirin (325 mg).
    3. Nitroglycerin Administration
      • Does the patient have nitroglycerin prescribed by the doctor?
        • If YES: Has the patient taken Viagra or something similar in the past 48 hours?
          • If YES: Do not give nitroglycerin.
      • Has the patient taken their nitroglycerin?
        • If YES: How many?
        • If NO: Have the patient sit down and take as the physician directed.
    4. Gather the patient's medications, if any.
