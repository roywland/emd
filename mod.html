OVERDOSE / POISONING

  • If not breathing, determine appropriate age group and go to CPR.
    [ ADULT CPR --- CHILD CPR --- INFANT CPR ]
  • If unconscious, go to AIRWAY CONTROL (Non-trauma).
  • Keep patient in area/house, if safe.
  • Get container of substance taken if at the scene.
  • Don't force coffee or place the patient in the shower.
  • Give nothing by mouth, including Ipecac, unless advised by poison control.
