DROWNING / DIVING ACCIDENT / WATER INJURY

  • Do not attempt to rescue the patient.
  • If unconscious, go to AIRWAY CONTROL (Trauma).
  • If unconscious/not breathing, go to CPR for the appropriate age group.
    [ ADULT CPR --- CHILD CPR --- INFANT CPR ]
  • Keep patient warm.
  • Do not move the patient around.
  • Gather the patient's medications, if any.
