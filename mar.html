ALLERGIC REACTION

  • Have the patient rest in the most comfortable position, loosen clothing, remove any jewelry.
  • Keep the neck straight -- remove any pillows.
  • Keep calm.
  • Brush off the stinger, if possible.
  • Ice the sting.
  • Gather any medications that the patient is taking , if any.
  • Does the patient have a reaction kit?
  • If YES, has it been used?
  • Use as the physician has directed.
