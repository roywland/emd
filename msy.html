UNCONSCIOUS / UNRESPONSIVE / SYNCOPE / FAINTING

  • If unconscious, go to AIRWAY CONTROL (Non-trauma).
  • If not breathing normally, determine appropriate age group and go to CPR instructions.
    [ ADULT CPR --- CHILD CPR --- INFANT CPR ]
  • Have the patient lie down.
  • If the patient is vomiting, lie the patient on their side.
  • Do not leave the patient. Be prepared for CPR.
  • Gather the patient's medications, if any.
